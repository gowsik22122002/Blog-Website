The Golden Hour

Published: May 15, 2025 | Category: Photography Tips

The golden hour is the period shortly after sunrise and before sunset when the sun sits low in the sky, casting a warm, diffused glow over everything it touches. Photographers cherish this time for its soft, golden light that naturally flatters skin tones, enhances colors, and creates gentle, elongated shadows.

Why Photographers Love It

It's a favorite for portraits, landscapes, and urban photography alike, adding atmosphere and depth to even the simplest of shots. During the golden hour, everyday scenes transform into cinematic frames filled with warmth and texture.

Tips for Golden Hour Photography

  • Plan your shoot location in advance
  • Use a wide aperture for dreamy bokeh effects
  • Experiment with backlighting and silhouettes
  • Bring a reflector to fill in shadows

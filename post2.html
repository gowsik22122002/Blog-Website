City Lights at Night

Published: May 10, 2025 | Category: Urban Photography

When darkness falls, cities come alive in a different light. Neon signs, shimmering reflections on wet streets, and the blur of headlights create a rich, dynamic canvas for photographers. Night photography captures the energy and elegance of urban spaces after dark, revealing scenes invisible by day.

The Magic of Long Exposures

Long exposure techniques turn moving cars into streaks of light, while illuminated skyscrapers become glowing pillars against the night sky. Every corner offers a new story â€” from quiet alleys to bustling avenues, the city never truly sleeps.

Essential Night Photography Gear

  • Sturdy tripod to prevent camera shake
  • Fast lens (f/2.8 or wider)
  • Remote shutter release or timer
  • Lens hood to reduce flare

Camera Settings for Night Shots

Scenario Aperture Shutter Speed ISO
Cityscapes f/8-f/11 10-30 sec 100-400
Light trails f/11-f/16 30+ sec 100
Handheld f/2.8 1/30-1/60 1600+
